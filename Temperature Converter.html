FORMULAE:

Celsius to Farenheit:
℉ = 9/5℃+32		 Celsius to Kelvin:
K = °C + 273
Farenheit to Celsius:
℃ = (℉-32)÷1.8		 Farenheit to Kelvin:
K = ((℉-32)÷1.8) + 273.15
Kelvin to Farenheit:
℉= 9/5(K-273.15)+32		 Kelvin to Celsius:
°C = K-273.15
